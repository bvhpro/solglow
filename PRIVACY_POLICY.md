# Privacy Policy for SolGlow

**Effective Date: January 16, 2026**

## Overview

SolGlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application for setting NFT wallpapers on Android devices.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Wallet Connection**: When you connect your Solana wallet, we temporarily access your public wallet address to fetch NFT metadata
- **App Preferences**: Wallpaper rotation settings, selected NFTs, and app configuration preferences

### Information Automatically Collected
- **NFT Metadata**: We fetch publicly available NFT information from your wallet via Helius API
- **Price Data**: We retrieve market prices for NFT collections from public APIs for the "Glow" effect feature
- **Device Information**: Basic Android device capabilities for wallpaper functionality

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Private Keys**: We never access, store, or transmit your wallet private keys
- **Personal Identification**: No names, emails, phone numbers, or personal identifiers
- **Financial Transactions**: We do not process payments or financial transactions
- **Location Data**: No geographical or location tracking
- **Biometric Data**: No fingerprints, facial recognition, or biometric information

## How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:
- Display your owned NFTs in the app gallery
- Set selected NFTs as device wallpapers
- Provide price-based visual effects ("Glow" feature)
- Maintain app preferences and settings
- Ensure app functionality and performance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- App preferences are stored locally on your device
- No user data is transmitted to external servers beyond necessary API calls
- Cached NFT images are stored temporarily in app cache

### Third-Party APIs
We interact with these services to provide app functionality:
- **Helius API**: For fetching NFT metadata (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Price APIs**: For market data (CoinGecko, Tensor - subject to their policies)
- **Solana RPC**: For blockchain data access

### Security Measures
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is permanently stored
- Wallet connections use established Solana wallet adapter protocols

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell, rent, or trade your information
- Share data with advertisers or marketers  
- Use your data for analytics or tracking
- Transfer data to third parties except for essential API functionality

## Your Rights and Choices

### You Can:
- Disconnect your wallet at any time
- Clear app data and cache through Android settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- Contact us about data practices

### Data Retention
- Wallet connection data is cleared when you disconnect
- App preferences persist until you clear app data or uninstall
- Cached images are automatically cleared based on device storage management

## Children's Privacy

SolGlow is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
